Enemies are invading the space! Help the astronaut survive


How To Play?

-Avoid contact with fish enemies by trying to shoot them.
-You can move up and down using up and down arrows on your keyboard.
-Ammos recharge with a speed of 3 ammos/second
-In order to survive you should get a score of 80 during 30 seconds of playing.

Power ups

Not all fish are bad... If you're lucky enough you'll get the lucky fish!
You can either kill it and get 15 points or pick it up to get powerups.
